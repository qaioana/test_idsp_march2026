# ApartmentOS Backlog

## Work Items

### #1 — Admin Bill Management
**Epic:** Cost Management
**Feature:** Bill Creation & Splitting

**Description:**
Allow the administrator to create bills by category (e.g. maintenance, utilities) for a given month, set a total amount, and choose a split method — either equally across all apartments or proportionally by apartment area (sqm). Admins can also mark individual apartments as paid and add notes to each bill.

---

### #2 — Resident Water Reading & Bill Tracker
**Epic:** Resident Portal
**Feature:** Water Readings & Bill History

**Description:**
Allow residents to log in with their apartment credentials, submit their monthly water meter readings, and view their personal bill history — including which bills are paid or outstanding.
